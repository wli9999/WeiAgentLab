## Risks

- Shorts performance can remain volatile beyond the first 72 hours.
- Changing several major variables at once can make the result difficult to interpret.
- Subscriber growth cannot be guaranteed; the project must learn from both positive and negative outcomes.
- Copyright and translation provenance must be checked for externally sourced material.
- ChatGPT Plus usage is limited; unnecessary browser, generation, and repeated-render work must be avoided.
- Every Short requires documented copyright, religion, race and identity, child-safety, and general viewer-safety review before upload.
- Any YouTube warning, claim, legal issue, privacy complaint, or serious safety complaint immediately stops publication and pauses related uploads pending human review.
